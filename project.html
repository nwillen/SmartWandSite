Comp 523: Smart Wand

Project Team Journal Deliverables

Smart Wand Summary

The TorchTech Smart Pelvic Wand mobile application calibrates with the Smart Pelvic Wand to allow users to control their apparatus through their mobile device while providing educational and progress tracking features.

Smart Wand Description

TorchTech Industries developed a smart pelvic wand to address pelvic floor dysfunction (PFD), a medical condition in which pelvic floor muscles become weak or tight. The pelvic smart ward is a mechanical wand that provide individuals with PFD the access to pelvic floor physical therapy at home. Our goal is to develop an interactive mobile application for the purpose of improving the accessibility of pelvic floor physical therapy. An interactive application would supply those who suffer from PFD guidance through physical therapy exercises, contain an education feature that provides exercise programs, instructions of wand placement, and a health forum to be edited by clinicians and patients. The application will also allow users to create personal accounts that store bio-based data from the wand after every therapy session providing a tracking system for progress, live biofeedback and a mapping of the pelvic floor muscles.
